Characters

#1 Tia Landry: Tia is the twin sister of Tamera. She was adopted by Lisa Landry and then moved into Ray's house. Tia is a straight A student and is usually responsible and mature (until Tamera gets in her head). She still enjoys her time and does fun things with her sister and friends. But, she is often having to keep track of her sister.
#2 Tamera Campbell: Tamera is the twin sister of Tia and was adopted by Ray Campbell. Tamera does not care about school that much, is always thinking about boys, and often makes bad decisions. She likes doing what she wants and having fun doing it. At some point however, she can be mature if she wants to.
#3 Lisa Landry: Lisa is Tia's adoptive mother and a dressmaker. She often makes bad decisions and is very outgoing. She likes to argue with Ray and not listen to anyone else. She also is always going out on dates and reminds me somewhat of Tamera. She allows the girls a lot of freedom to do what they want.
#4 Ray Campbell: Ray is Tamera's adoptive father and runs a limo service. He likes everything a specific way and is more controlling of the girls. He argues with Lisa a lot! Later in the series, he actually gets more involved with politics.

These are the main four characters that are in every episode. Some other characters that come up are Roger, who is their next door neighbor and friend. Also, Jordan who is Tamera's boyfriend for the last couple of seasons. Lastly, Tyreke who is Tia's boyfriend/friend for the last couple of seasons.
