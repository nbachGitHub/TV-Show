Sister, Sister

Overview

Sister, Sister is an American Sitcom released in the 1990's. It has a total of 6 seasons, and I am on the last season and have almost completed the entire show. It is available on Netflix.

About

Tia (Tia Mowry) and Tamera (Tamera Mowry) are twins who are seperated at birth. Tamera is then adopted by Ray Campbell (Tim Reid) and Tia is adopted by Lisa Landry (Jackee Harry). They aren't reunited until they are in high school at the mall. After they get to know eachother, the parents agree to move in to Ray's house so the twins could be together. The twins look alike, but are very different inside, the adoptive parents also don't get along at first. This story follows the twins' experiences through high school and their first year of college. They go through normal experiences like any teen, but with a humorous twist.
